AI Content & Data Processing

Markd utilizes advanced AI technology (powered by Google Gemini) to assist in scanning and parsing timetables from images or PDF files. When you use the "Scan Timetable" feature:

  • Images or documents you provide are transmitted to Google's servers for processing.
  • This data is used solely to extract text and generate schedule entries for your app.
  • Processing is handled securely in accordance with the Google AI Terms of Service.

  • Notifications: To send reminders about upcoming classes and schedule changes.
