Markd

Ultimate Smart Attendance & Timetable Manager

📖 Built with privacy and efficiency in mind, Markd eliminates the need for manual calculations helping you stay above your required attendance criteria no matter how unpredictable your semester gets.

✨ Key Features

🤖
AI Timetable Scanner

Instantly read and digitize your timetable from photos or PDFs. Automatically extracts subjects, class timings, teachers and assigned classrooms in seconds.

📊
Comprehensive Tracking

Mark exact status for any lecture: Present, Absent, Holiday or use On Duty for official events to protect your overall attendance.

➕
Extra Class Support

Semester schedules rarely stay static. Easily add an Extra Class for makeup lectures to ensure your log perfectly reflects your hours.

🎯
"Safe to Bunk" Predictor

Set a custom target and Markd calculates exactly how many upcoming classes you can safely miss without dropping below your threshold.

📅
Interactive Calendar

The dynamic calendar view clearly highlights days with scheduled classes and provides a detailed breakdown of your daily timetable.

📈
Detailed Analytics

Visual charts detail overall attendance, subject breakdowns and weekly trends to help focus on subjects that require more attention.

🔔
Proactive Alerts

Stop relying on memory. Get smart notifications right before each scheduled class begins, prompting you to mark your status.

🔒
100% Private Storage

No accounts, no logins, no cloud syncing. All your academic data is securely stored entirely locally on your device.

💾
Export & Backup

Export complete history to Excel or PDF format. Easily create local backup files to seamlessly transfer between devices.

🎨
Modern Design

Clean, intuitive interface fully supporting both light and dark modes with customizable time formatting options.
